# Sustainify Roadmap

## Vision

---

## Near Term (Next 3-6 Months)

### Intelligent Policy Monitoring
- Automated monitoring of regulatory news, government databases, and industry publications
- Smart matching of policy changes to company profiles (industry, location, size, emissions)
- Push notifications for relevant regulations with impact assessment and compliance timelines
- Visual regulatory calendar showing upcoming deadlines

### Enhanced Dashboard
- Drag-and-drop customizable widgets
- Role-based views (executive, operations, compliance)
- PDF and CSV export for audit-ready documentation
- Mobile-responsive design

### Extended Metrics
- Water usage tracking and waste management
- Energy consumption breakdown by source
- Supply chain emissions (Scope 3) tracking

---

## Mid Term (6-12 Months)

### Integrations & Automation
- Connect to accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks)
- Direct data feeds from utility providers
- Cloud provider carbon footprint integration (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Shipping and logistics emissions tracking

### Predictive Compliance
- ML models forecasting which regulations will affect your business
- Impact and cost estimation before regulations take effect
- Step-by-step preparation roadmaps with timelines
- Benchmarking against similar businesses

### Automated Reporting
- One-click compliance reports (GRI, SASB, CDP standards)
- Automatic audit trail documentation
- Certification prep workflows (B Corp, ISO 14001)
- Customizable stakeholder reports

---

## Long Term (12+ Months)

### Full ESG Platform
- Social metrics (diversity, community impact, labor practices)
- Governance tracking (board composition, ethics policies)
- Comprehensive ESG scoring with improvement recommendations

### Advanced Features
- ROI calculator for sustainability initiatives
- Scenario modeling for compliance strategies
- AI-powered cost optimization recommendations
- Multi-location and subsidiary management
- Team collaboration tools with task management

### Global Expansion
- International regulation support (EU, UK, APAC)
- Multi-language platform
- Regional partnerships with local auditors and providers

---

## How We Prioritize

1. **Regulatory Impact** - Helps businesses avoid fines or meet requirements
2. **Cost Savings** - Reduces expenses or increases efficiency
3. **User Demand** - Features customers are asking for
4. **Technical Feasibility** - Can build it well with current resources

---

This roadmap evolves based on user feedback, market conditions, and regulatory changes. Have ideas? Open an issue on GitHub or reach out to the team.
